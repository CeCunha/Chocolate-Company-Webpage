1990

The Chocolate Drop company is founded by a young chocolatier named Thomas. He begins making and selling artisanal chocolates in his small shop in a quaint town. In 2000, The Chocolate Drop expands, and Thomas opens several more locations throughout the region. The company's reputation for quality and delicious chocolate spreads, and they start selling their products in other stores and online.

2010

Thomas begins experimenting with new flavors and ingredients, including exotic spices and fruits. They also launch a line of dairy-free and vegan chocolates, which quickly become popular with health-conscious customers. In 2020, the company continues to grow and innovate, introducing sustainable and ethically sourced chocolate. They partner with cocoa farmers and invest in fair trade practices to ensure that their products are not only delicious, but also responsible and environmentally friendly.

2022

In response to the pandemic, The Chocolate Drop pivots to e-commerce and delivery, allowing customers to order their chocolates online and have them shipped directly to their homes. They also create special "quarantine" chocolate collections to help people get through difficult times.

We continue to thrive, with new stores and products launching every year. We remain committed to sustainability and ethical practices, and we are recognized as one of the most respected and innovative chocolate companies in the world.
